One of the most exciting aspect of planning your wedding cake is deciding what your cake will taste like. I believe that all the wedding cakes we create must taste as good as it looks!

All our cakes have 4 layers of light and fluffy delicious sponge paired with fresh fruit reductions or salted caramel or buttercreams or luscious curds. All our cake tiers are about 5 inches tall and are frosted with Couverture White/Dark Chocolate Ganache Frosting and iced with a layer of good quality sugarpaste.

Classic Flavours

  • Vanilla Bean Cake with Vanilla Bean Buttercream with Raspberry Reduction.
  • Rich Chocolate Cake with Chocolate Buttercream
  • Red Velvette Cake with White Chocolate Buttercream
  • Caramel Cake with Salted Caramel Filling and Caramel Buttercream
  • Lemon Cake with Lemon Curd Filling and Lemon Buttercream
  • Pistachio Cake with Rose Buttercream and Raspberry Reduction
  • Biscoff Crusted Cake with Biscoff filling and Biscoff Buttercream

Signature Flavours

  • Strawberry Cake with Strawberry filling and Strawberry Buttercream
  • Coffee Cake with Toffee Crunch filling and Mascarpone Buttercream
  • Coconut Almond Cake with Coconut Buttercream
  • Mango Cake with Passionfruit Curd filling and Raspberry Buttercream
  • Pineapple Coconut Cake with Pineapple Coconut filling and Rum Flavoured Buttercream
