MEET JANU

I’m Janannie Rangaswamy,

The creative soul behind the edible artistry you’ll find here. With a heart that’s part coder and part confectioner, I’ve embarked on a delicious journey from software engineer to cake artist.

Every Cake I create is a symphony of flavours and textures, a canvas for celebrations and joy. From elegantly minimalist designs to whimsical, fantastical creations, my portfolio reflects the fusion of precision and playfulness that defines my style.
