Our PAckage

I enjoy working closely with clients to design custom cakes tailored to specific themes, events or personal preferences. I offer wedding cakes, classic birthday cakes, over the top sculpted/3D cakes, Fondant and Sugar work, cupcakes and sweet treats. Every cake I create tastes as good as it looks.

I’m based in Basingstoke, registered with the Council and fully insured.
